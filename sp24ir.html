Week 5

Dual Steenrod Algebra
\(\mathcal{A}^*:=A^{\vee }\) is defined to be the dual Hopf algebra to \(\mathcal{A}\). \(\mathcal{A}\) is cocommutative algebra, so \(\mathcal{A}_*\) is commutative Hopf algebra.

Define \(\xi_i\in \mathcal{A}^*\) to be dual to \(\mathop{\mathrm{Sq}}^{2^{i-1} ,\dots,2,1}\). Thus \(\deg (\xi_i)=2^i-1\).

The dual Steenrod algebra \(\mathcal{A}_*\) is a polynomial algebra, \[\mathcal{A}_* \cong \mathbb{F}_2\left[z_1, z_2, \ldots\right],\] where \(z_i\) is dual to \(\mathop{\mathrm{Sq}}^{2^{i-1} ,\dots,2,1}\) and has degree \(2^i-1\). The coproduct is given by \[\Delta\left(z_n\right)=\sum_{0 \leq k \leq n} z_{n-k}^{2^k} \otimes z_k\] and \[\chi\left(z_n\right)=\sum_{\alpha \in \operatorname{Comp}(n)} z_{a_1}^{2^{\sigma(1)}} \cdots z_{a_{\ell}(\alpha)}^{2^{\sigma(\ell(\alpha))}}\] where \(\sigma(k)=\sum_{j<k} a_j\)

cohomology of Hopf algebra let \(\mathcal{H}\) be a Hopf algebra over a field \(k\). Define \[H^n(\mathcal{H})=\mathrm{Ext }^n_{\mathcal{H}}(k,k)\] We define \[\mathcal{A}(n)_*=\mathcal{A}_* /\left(z_1^{2^{n+1}}, z_2^{2^n}, \ldots, z_{n+1}^2, z_{n+2}, \ldots\right) \cong \frac{\mathbb{F}_2\left[z_1, \ldots, z_{n+1}\right]}{\left(z_1^{2^{n+1}}, z_2^{2^n}, \ldots, z_{n+1}^2\right) }\]This is an algebra of dimension \(2 \frac{(n+1)(n+2)}{2}\), with top degree element in degree \(2^{n+2}(n-1)+n+5\).

\(h_{i,n}\coloneqq \xi_i^{2^n}\) The dual \(\mathcal{A}(n)\) of \(\mathcal{A}(n)_*\) is the subalgebra of \(\mathcal{A}\) generated by \(\mathop{\mathrm{Sq}}^{1,2,4, \ldots, 2^n}\).1
